How to Apply

Follow these steps to submit your tender application

Application Process

1

Prepare Your Documents

Gather all required documents including company registration, tax clearance, trading license, and financial statements.

2

Complete Application Form

Download and complete the tender application form available at the Town Council offices or on our website.

3

Submit Your Proposal

Submit your completed application with all supporting documents to the Procurement Office at Kayunga Town Council.

4

Evaluation Process

Applications will be evaluated by the procurement committee based on set criteria and qualifications.

5

Award Notification

Successful bidders will be notified and contracts will be awarded within 30 days of application deadline.
