Our Health Services

Comprehensive public health services for the Kayunga community

Food Safety & Hygiene

Ensuring all food establishments comply with health standards to prevent foodborne illnesses and protect public health through comprehensive inspection, training, and monitoring programs.

Inspection & Licensing

  • Regular inspections of restaurants, hotels, and food vendors
  • Issuance of food handler's certificates
  • Pre-operational inspections for new businesses
  • Compliance monitoring with food safety standards

Training & Education

  • Mandatory food safety training programs
  • Certification for food establishment managers
  • Hygiene education for proper food handling
  • HACCP principles training

Quality Monitoring

  • Food sampling and contamination testing
  • Storage temperature monitoring
  • Expiry date and labeling compliance
  • Food source verification

Environmental Sanitation

Monitoring sanitation facilities and waste management systems to maintain a clean and healthy environment for all residents through regular inspections and enforcement of sanitation standards.

Facility Inspection

  • Public sanitation facility monitoring
  • Waste management system oversight
  • Public cleanliness enforcement
  • Drainage system maintenance

Standards Enforcement

  • Public toilet facility standards
  • Waste disposal compliance
  • Sanitation code enforcement
  • Environmental health regulations

Disease Prevention & Control

Surveillance, prevention, and control of communicable diseases in the community through vaccination programs, outbreak response, and public health education initiatives.

Vaccination Programs

  • Regular immunization campaigns
  • Child and adult vaccination services
  • Outbreak response vaccination
  • Vaccine-preventable disease control

Disease Control

  • Disease outbreak response teams
  • Epidemiological surveillance
  • Vector control programs
  • Infection prevention measures

Waste Management

Comprehensive waste management services to maintain cleanliness, prevent environmental pollution, and promote public health through proper collection, disposal, and recycling initiatives.

Collection Services

  • Regular household waste collection
  • Commercial waste management
  • Special waste collection programs
  • Hazardous waste handling

Recycling & Disposal

  • Community recycling programs
  • Waste segregation education
  • Landfill management
  • Composting initiatives

Environmental Protection

  • Illegal dumping prevention
  • Waste reduction campaigns
  • Environmental impact monitoring
  • Clean-up community programs
