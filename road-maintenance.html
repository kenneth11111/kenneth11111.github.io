Our Road Services

Comprehensive road infrastructure management services for Kayunga Town Council

Road Opening

Construction of new road networks to improve connectivity and access to underserved areas. This includes land clearing, grading, and initial road formation to create new transport corridors that enhance mobility and economic development.

Routine Maintenance

Regular inspection and maintenance activities to preserve road conditions and prevent deterioration. Includes pothole patching, drainage clearing, surface repairs, and preventive maintenance to extend the lifespan of our road infrastructure.

Public Infrastructure Maintenance & Construction

Development and maintenance of essential public infrastructure including drainage systems, culverts, retaining walls, and other road-related structures. Ensures proper water management and structural integrity of the road network.
