Executive Leadership

The team responsible for the strategic direction and day-to-day operations of Kayunga Town Council

Hon. John M. Ssebuliba

Town Mayor

Hon. John M. Ssebuliba has been serving as the Town Mayor of Kayunga since 2021. With over 15 years of experience in public service and community development, he has been instrumental in driving infrastructure projects, improving service delivery, and fostering economic growth in Kayunga. His leadership focuses on transparency, accountability, and inclusive development that benefits all residents.

mayor@kayungatc.go.ug

Hon. Robert K. Mutyaba

Deputy Mayor

Hon. Robert K. Mutyaba serves as the Deputy Mayor, supporting the mayor in council activities and representing the town in various forums. With a background in education and community mobilization, he has been pivotal in implementing youth empowerment programs and improving educational facilities across Kayunga.

+256 772 654 321
deputymayor@kayungatc.go.ug

Ms. Sarah N. Kigozi

Town Clerk

Ms. Sarah N. Kigozi is the Town Clerk responsible for the day-to-day administration of Kayunga Town Council. With over 12 years of experience in local government administration, she oversees the implementation of council decisions, manages human resources, and ensures efficient service delivery to residents.
