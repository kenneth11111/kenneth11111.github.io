Our Commitment to Kayunga

Kayunga Town Council (KTC) is dedicated to improving the quality of life for all residents through effective governance, sustainable development, and community engagement.

Established to coordinate town services and infrastructure projects, KTC has been at the forefront of urban development in the region, focusing on creating clean, safe, and progressive urban centers.

Our Mission

To create clean, safe and progressive urban centers through effective service delivery and community participation.

Our Vision

A thriving municipality with inclusive services, strong infrastructure, and empowered communities.
