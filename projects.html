Implementation Phase

Solar Street Lighting Project

Installation of solar-powered street lights across major roads and public spaces in Kayunga Town to enhance security, improve night-time visibility, and promote sustainable energy use.

Project Details

  • Installation of 200 solar-powered street lights
  • Coverage of 5km of major town roads
  • Focus on high-traffic areas and security hotspots
  • Use of high-efficiency LED lighting technology
  • Automatic dusk-to-dawn operation
  • Battery backup for continuous operation during cloudy days
  • Low maintenance and operational costs
  • Reduction in carbon emissions
Budget:
350 Million UGX
Status:
Implementation Ongoing

Current Status: Phase 1 completed with 80 lights installed along Mumyuka Road and Hospital Road. Phase 2 implementation underway, targeting market areas and residential zones.
