Finance Department

Mr. James K. Mugisha - Director

Responsible for financial management, budgeting, revenue collection, and fiscal planning to ensure sustainable development of Kayunga Town Council.

Department Overview

The Finance Department is responsible for the efficient management of the Town Council's financial resources. We ensure transparency, accountability, and proper utilization of public funds to support development projects and service delivery throughout Kayunga.

Key Responsibilities

Budget Management

Preparation, execution, and monitoring of annual budgets in alignment with council priorities

Revenue Collection

Efficient collection of local revenues including taxes, fees, and other municipal charges

Financial Reporting

Preparation of accurate financial statements and reports for stakeholders and regulatory bodies

Grant Management

Coordination and management of grants from government and development partners

Services Offered

Tax Collection Services

Budget Consultation

Financial Reporting

Grant Management
