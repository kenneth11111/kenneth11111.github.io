Kayunga Town Council Embraces Digital Revenue Administration Through IRAS

May 29, 2023 Kayunga Town Council Digital Transformation

Strengthening Revenue Collection Through Technology

Kayunga Town Council has embraced the Integrated Revenue Administration System (IRAS) as part of its commitment to improving revenue collection, accountability, and service delivery within the municipality.

The adoption of IRAS marks a major milestone in the Town Council’s digital transformation agenda aimed at modernizing local government operations and enhancing efficiency in revenue administration.

What is IRAS?

The Integrated Revenue Administration System (IRAS) is a digital platform designed to streamline assessment, billing, collection, and management of local government revenues in a transparent and accountable manner.

Through the system, taxpayers and business owners can now access revenue services more conveniently while reducing paperwork and minimizing delays associated with manual processes.

According to the Town Council leadership, the implementation of IRAS is expected to increase local revenue performance, improve monitoring of collections, and support informed decision-making through real-time reporting.

The Council continues to encourage residents, market vendors, and business operators to cooperate with the new digital system for efficient and transparent revenue management.

Kayunga Town Council remains committed to innovation and the adoption of modern technologies that enhance public service delivery and contribute towards sustainable urban development.
