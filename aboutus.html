The Authority

The Authority is a corporate body with perpetual succession and may sue and be sued in its corporate name...

Overview

The Authority is the governing body of the Capital City and administers Capital City on behalf of the central government subject to this Act.

Composition of Authority

Members include:
1. The Lord Mayor;
2. The Deputy Lord Mayor;
3. One councillor directly elected by secret ballot to represent each electoral area in the Capital City; and others as provided by the law.

Functions of Lord Mayor

The Lord Mayor is the political head of the Capital City, presides over meetings of the Authority, performs ceremonial duties and provides strategic leadership.

Administrative divisions

Kampala is divided into five divisions (Central, Kawempe, Makindye, Nakawa & Rubaga), each headed by a popularly elected Mayor.
