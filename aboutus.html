About the Authority

Last updated: Oct 17, 2025

The Authority

The Authority is a corporate body with perpetual succession and may sue and be sued in its corporate name. It is the governing body of the Capital City and administers the Capital City on behalf of the central government subject to the enabling Act.

Composition of the Authority

  • The Lord Mayor
  • The Deputy Lord Mayor
  • Councillors representing electoral areas
  • Representatives of youth and persons with disabilities
  • Women councillors forming one third of the Authority

Functions of Lord Mayor

The Lord Mayor is the political head of the Capital City, presides over meetings of the Authority, and represents the City in ceremonial and civic matters.

Administrative divisions

Kampala is divided into five divisions: Central, Kawempe, Makindye, Nakawa & Rubaga.
