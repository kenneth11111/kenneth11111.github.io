Finance Department

Department Overview

The Finance Department is responsible for the efficient management of the Town Council's financial resources. We ensure transparency, accountability, and proper utilization of public funds to support development projects and service delivery.

Key Responsibilities

  • Budget preparation, execution, and monitoring
  • Revenue collection and management
  • Financial reporting and accountability
  • Revenue enhancement and compliance enforcement
  • Financial planning and forecasting

Services Offered

Tax Collection

Budget Consultation

Financial Reporting

Administration Department

Department Overview

The Administration Department serves as the backbone of Kayunga Town Council, ensuring smooth operations across all departments. We manage human resources, information technology systems, and coordinate with law enforcement agencies to maintain order and efficiency.

Key Responsibilities

  • Human resources management and development
  • Information technology systems management
  • Records and document management
  • Law enforcement coordination
  • Office administration and management
  • Staff welfare and development

Services Offered

Staff Recruitment

IT Support

Document Processing

Security Coordination

Works Department

Department Overview

The Works Department is responsible for the planning, construction, and maintenance of all public infrastructure in Kayunga Town Council. Our team of engineers and technicians work tirelessly to ensure that roads, buildings, and public facilities meet the highest standards of quality and safety.

Key Responsibilities

  • Road construction and maintenance
  • Building construction and renovation
  • Urban planning and development control
  • Public infrastructure maintenance
  • Drainage and sanitation works
  • Project management and supervision

Services Offered

Road Maintenance

Construction Oversight

Urban Planning

Infrastructure Repair

Production Department

Department Overview

The Production Department drives agricultural and economic development initiatives in Kayunga. We support local farmers, promote sustainable production methods, and facilitate value addition to enhance economic opportunities for residents.

Key Responsibilities

  • Agricultural extension services
  • Livestock and fisheries development
  • Value addition and agro-processing
  • Market linkages and access
  • Farmer training and capacity building
  • Cooperative development support

Services Offered

Agricultural Support

Production Training

Farm Equipment

Market Access
