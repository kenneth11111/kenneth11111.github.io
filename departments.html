Finance Department

Department Overview

The Finance Department is responsible for the efficient management of the Town Council's financial resources. We ensure transparency, accountability, and proper utilization of public funds to support development projects and service delivery.

Key Responsibilities

  • Budget preparation, execution, and monitoring
  • Revenue collection and management
  • Financial reporting and accountability
  • Payroll management and staff remuneration
  • Grant management and donor coordination
  • Financial planning and forecasting
